Disaster-Shield

In Case of Earthquake


1. Drop, Cover, and Hold On: During shaking, drop to the ground, take cover under sturdy furniture, and hold on until the shaking stops.

2. Stay Indoors: If indoors, stay away from windows, walls, and glass objects. Avoid using elevators.

3. Move to Open Space Outdoors: If outside, move to an open area away from buildings, trees, and utility wires.

4. Have an Emergency Kit: Keep an emergency kit with essentials like water, non-perishable food, first aid supplies, and a flashlight.

5. Secure Heavy Items: Fasten heavy furniture and appliances to walls to prevent them from falling during an earthquake.

6. Know Safe Spots: Identify safe spots in each room, such as under sturdy tables or against an interior wall.

7. Stay Informed: Keep an eye on official warnings, and only return to your home when it is declared safe.
