Disaster-Shield

In Case of Lightning


1. Seek Shelter Immediately: When you hear thunder, move indoors or into a hard-topped vehicle immediately.

2. Avoid Water and Electronics: Do not use water (showers, faucets) or electronic devices like phones or computers during a storm.

3. Stay Away from Windows: Avoid standing near windows, doors, or other open areas during lightning.

4. Stay Indoors Until Safe: Remain indoors for at least 30 minutes after the last clap of thunder.

5. Do Not Shelter Under Trees: If outdoors, never take shelter under a tree or other tall object that may attract lightning.

6. Unplug Electronics: Disconnect electronics to protect them from power surges caused by lightning strikes.

7. Avoid Open Fields: Stay away from open fields, hills, and areas where you are the tallest object.
