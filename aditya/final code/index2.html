Disaster-Shield

In Case of Flood


1- Monitor Weather Alerts: Stay informed with flood warnings from local authorities, weather apps, or news channels.

2- Move to Higher Ground: Evacuate low-lying and flood-prone areas immediately and move to a safer, elevated location.

3- Avoid Walking or Driving Through Floodwaters: Even shallow water can be dangerous. Avoid walking or driving in flooded areas as water depth is hard to assess.

4- Secure Valuables: Move important items, documents, and electronics to higher ground to protect them from water damage.

5- Prepare an Emergency Kit: Have an emergency kit with essentials like water, non-perishable food, a first aid kit, flashlights, batteries, and important documents.

6- Turn Off Gas, Electricity, and Water: In case of flooding, turn off gas and electricity to prevent fires and electrocution.
