Disaster-Shield

In Case of Cyclone


1. Monitor Warnings: Stay updated with official weather alerts and evacuation orders.

2. Evacuate if Advised: If local authorities recommend evacuation, leave the area immediately and move to a safe location.

3. Secure Your Home: Reinforce windows, doors, and roofs to withstand strong winds. Remove outdoor objects that could become projectiles.

4. Prepare an Emergency Kit: Keep essential supplies, including food, water, a first aid kit, and necessary medications.

5. Avoid Floodwaters: Do not walk or drive through flooded areas, as it can be dangerous and cause drowning.

6. Stay Indoors: During the cyclone, stay in a secure room away from windows and doors.

7. Wait for Official Clearance: Do not return to your home until authorities declare it safe.
